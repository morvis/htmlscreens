Dining Options

All food served during the conference, with the exception of certain retail outlets in the Marriott Marquis, is kosher and supervised by the Capitol-K Rabbinical Council of Greater Washington. Your personalized schedule will show if any of your scheduled meetings and programs include meals.

Washington Convention Center: A wide range of food and beverages will be available for purchase using a credit card or meal voucher. Vouchers for breakfast and lunch may be purchased at the Conference Concierge in the AIPAC Village, which will accept cash and credit cards.

Within the AIPAC Village, look for two Village Marketplaces where breakfast, fresh salads, sandwiches, hot entrées, snacks and beverages will be available for purchase by credit card or voucher.

Uptown Food District will offer breakfast, fresh salads, sandwiches, hot entrées, snacks and beverages.

Coffee and snacks will be available for purchase at the AIPAC Coffee House, village snack carts, and multiple locations inside the Washington Convention Center.
