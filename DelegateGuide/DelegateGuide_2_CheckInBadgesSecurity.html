Delegate Check-In

Badge Mailing: Most delegates will receive their badges by mail before the conference. Badges are mailed individually, even when being sent to the same household. Therefore, badges may arrive on different days.

Badges will not be mailed to delegates with mailing addresses outside of the United States.

Badge Pick-Up: If you have not received your badge in the mail, forgotten it at home, or need to make a change to your badge, please visit the Delegate Badge Pick-Up Desk in Salon F of the Washington Convention Center. Please enter the Washington Convention Center using the doors at 7th Street, NW and Mt. Vernon Place.

Badges may only be picked up during the scheduled times listed below. You must claim your badge in person; a family member or friend cannot pick it up on your behalf.

All children must be registered and must wear a Policy Conference badge. High school delegates who do not have a government-issued photo ID may present a current school-issued photo ID.

In the event your badge is lost or stolen, please visit the Delegate Badge Pick-Up Desk and we will void and re-print your badge. Once a badge is voided, it will no longer provide access to conference venues or events.

Security

The AIPAC Policy Conference takes security very seriously. Badges must be worn and visible at all times.

You must present your badge and a government-issued photo ID when entering any conference venue.

The name on your badge and your government-issued photo ID must be a match or near match.

To enter the Washington Convention Center, the Marriott Marquis and the Verizon Center all delegates must pass through magnetometers. Magnetometers are sensitive to metal. To ensure quicker entrance into conference venues, be prepared to empty your pockets. Place any pins on your lanyard for easy removal.

For security reasons, please do not photograph your badge or share it on social media at any time.

Protestors: Please do not engage with any protestors or detractors inside or outside Policy Conference venues — or on social media. Please ignore them and simply alert AIPAC staff or security.

U.S. Secret Service Security: Additional security may be added for General Sessions with high-level speakers. This secondary security, administered by the U.S. Secret Service, operates under strict rules that prohibit the following items:
  • Liquids
  • Laptops
  • Umbrellas
  • Luggage
  • Aerosol Sprays
  • Lighters
  • Matches
  • Box Cutters
  • Explosive Materials
  • Flammable Items
  • Tools
  • Pepper Spray/Mace
  • Sharp Objects
  • Pocket Knife
  • Food
Please Note:
U.S. Secret Service may prevent late-arriving delegates from entering into the venue. Delegates should allow additional time to clear security throughout the conference.

The list of prohibited items is subject to change without warning.


Entrances and Exits to Venues

The Washington Convention Center: The main entrance to the Washington Convention Center is located at 801 Mt. Vernon Place, NW between 7th Street, NW and 9th Street, NW.

Additional entrances are located on L Street, NW between 7th Street, NW and 9th Street, NW, at the corner of 7th Street, NW and Mt. Vernon Place, NW, and at the corner of M Street, NW and 7th Street, NW.

Delegates should feel free to enter at their most convenient location.

Your Policy Conference badge and a government-issued photo ID are required to enter. Delegates picking up or needing to make changes to their badges onsite should enter at 7th Street, NW and Mount Vernon Place, NW.

The Marriott Marquis Hotel: The main entrance to the Marriott Marquis Hotel is located at 901 Massachusetts Avenue, NW between 9th and 10th Streets, NW.

Starting at 3:00 p.m. on Saturday, access to the Marriott Marquis will be limited exclusively to Policy Conference delegates. Policy Conference badges and government-issued photo IDs are required upon entrance at this time. Guests staying at the Marriott Marquis on Saturday should plan to bring their badges with them if they leave the hotel after 3:00 p.m.

Once you pass through security at the Washington Convention Center or the Marriott Marquis, you will not be required to pass through security again as you move between the two venues via the underground connector.

The Verizon Center: The main entrance to the Verizon Center is located at 601 F Street, NW between 6th Street, NW and 7th Street, NW.

The Verizon Center is just four blocks from the Washington Convention Center; we encourage you to walk and enter the venue via the F Street entrance.

Shuttle buses from the Washington Convention Center and Marriott Marquis will pick-up and drop-off at designated points near the Verizon Center.

Policy Conference badges and government-issued photo IDs are required upon entrance.

Exits from the Verizon Center: Buses to Policy Conference hotels and the Washington Convention Center will be available at the end of the General Sessions and after the concert. We encourage delegates to walk to the nearby conference hotel.
