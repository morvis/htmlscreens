The AIPAC Village

When you need a moment to relax, grab some food or engage in interactive programming, stop by the AIPAC Village, the central gathering place and exhibition area at Policy Conference.

Village Hours
Saturday: 3:00 p.m. – 11:00 p.m.
Sunday: 7:00 a.m. – 7:00 p.m.
Monday: 6:30 a.m. – 3:00 p.m.

AIPAC Village Help Desks and Conference Concierge: Have a question? Visit the Help Desks in the AIPAC Village. Here you will find these helpful resources:
  • Conference Concierge
  • AIPAC Lobbying Help Desk
  • AIPAC Campus Help Desk
  • AIPAC App Support
  • "Join the Club" Membership Desk
Quick Stop Kiosks: Stop by and print your personalized schedule, choose your breakout sessions, and register for future events, including Policy Conference 2018. Quick Stop Kiosks will be conveniently located within the Village. Those who register on site for next year's conference will receive a $250 discount, available only during the conference!

Club Lounges: AIPAC lounges for club members will be located in the AIPAC Village.
  • Leadership Lounge: Available exclusively to Minyan, Chairman's Council, President's Cabinet, Senate Club, and AIPAC Partners for Tomorrow, the Leadership Lounge is an ideal place to relax, have a snack, and connect.
  • Congressional Club Lounge: Located in the AIPAC Village, the Congressional Club Lounge is the premier destination for AIPAC's Congressional Club members.
  • AIPAC Fellow Lounge: Inside, delegates will discover exciting opportunities for fresh minds to work for AIPAC.
AIPAC Literature Carts: Two mobile literature carts will be located inside the AIPAC Village. The carts will carry the PC Daily, pertinent lobbying information and policy publications.

Open-Air Stages: The AIPAC Village has two in-the-round stages featuring presentations by groundbreaking Israeli innovators and a diverse mix of AIPAC activists and experts on the issues. The stages will give presenters and participants the opportunity to engage in an interactive setting.

AIPAC Coffee House: Stop by the AIPAC Coffee House for a quick snack and conversation with a friend. Stay and participate in a breakout on the Coffee House stage. Also located in the AIPAC Coffee House is the AIPAC bookstore, which features titles by Policy Conference speakers.

Policy Conference Exhibits: Be sure to visit our world-class exhibitors, many of which you will see featured on the main stage during Policy Conference general sessions. In the Village, you will have the opportunity to get up close and hands-on with some of the finest examples of Israeli innovation and U.S.-Israel cooperation.

Village Marketplaces: Grab a bite to eat at one of two Village Marketplaces where kosher soups, salads, sandwiches, snacks and beverages will be available for purchase. The Village Marketplaces will accept credit cards and meal vouchers. Please note: Meal vouchers will be available for purchase with credit cards or cash at the Conference Concierge in the AIPAC Village. These vouchers can only be used at the Uptown Food District and the AIPAC Village Marketplaces in the Washington Convention Center.
