Delegates with Disabilities

AIPAC is dedicated to making Policy Conference safe and comfortable for all of our delegates.

Wheelchair Accommodations: There are a small number of wheelchairs and scooters available at the Washington Convention Center. You may check out a wheelchair or scooter each day at the Delegates with Disabilities Desk in the Grand Lobby. They are available on a first-come, first-served basis; therefore, we encourage you to make your own arrangements in advance through Scootaround Rentals at 1-888-441-7575.

There are a small number of wheelchairs available at the Verizon Center for transportation to your seat only. You may check out a transport wheelchair at the Guest Services Desk located on the 100 level concourse at section 102 or on the 400 level concourse at section 401.

American Sign Language Interpreter: All morning General Sessions at the Washington Convention Center will have ASL Interpreters available in Halls DE.

All evening General Sessions at the Verizon Center will have ASL Interpreters next to Loge Box 104, located behind section 104 on the 100 level.

Assistive Listening Devices (ALD): Assistive Listening Devices (ALD) will be available at each conference venue. Listed below are locations where you may retrieve an ALD. To reserve your ALD in each location, we will collect your government-issued photo ID and return it to you once you have returned the device after the session.

Washington Convention Center ALD pickup locations:

General Sessions: An ALD may be checked out at the Assistive Listening Device Desk in Halls DE, at which time we will collect your driver’s license. Please return the device to the same desk immediately after the show.

Breakout Sessions: An ALD may be checked out at the Delegates with Disabilities Desk in the Grand Lobby, at which time we will collect your driver’s license. Please return the device to the same desk after your last breakout session of the day.

Marriott Marquis ALD pickup locations:

Minyan Lunches: An ALD may be checked out at the technician table at the back of the Ballroom.

Breakout Sessions: You may check out an ALD at the Delegates with Disabilities Desk located on M1 across from the Howard University Room. We will collect your driver’s license. Please return the device after your last breakout session of the day in order to retrieve your license.

Verizon Center ALD pickup locations:

You may check out an ALD at the Guest Services desk located on the 100 level concourse at section 102 or on the 400 level concourse at section 401. Your photo ID will be collected at this time. Please return your device to the same Guest Services location after the General Session in order to retrieve your driver’s license.
