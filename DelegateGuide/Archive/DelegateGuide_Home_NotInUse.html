We are excited to welcome you to Washington, D.C. and to AIPAC’s 58th Policy Conference! These three days will be filled with powerful speeches by American and Israeli leaders, exclusive conversations with key decision makers, as well as unparalleled opportunities to dive into the U.S – Israel relationship, have your voice heard, and make a difference.
