Security

The AIPAC Policy Conference takes security very seriously. Badges must be worn and visible at all times.

You must present your badge and a government-issued photo ID when entering any conference venue.

The name on your badge and your government-issued photo ID must be a match or near match.

To enter the Washington Convention Center, the Marriott Marquis and the Verizon Center all delegates must pass through magnetometers. Magnetometers are sensitive to metal. To ensure quicker entrance into conference venues, be prepared to empty your pockets. Place any pins on your lanyard for easy removal.

For security reasons, please do not photograph your badge or share it on social media at any time.

Protestors
Please do not engage with any protestors or detractors inside or outside Policy Conference venues —or on social media. Please ignore them and simply alert AIPAC staff or security.

U.S. Secret Service Security
Additional security may be added for general sessions with high-level speakers. This secondary security, administered by the U.S. Secret Service, operates under strict rules that prohibit the following items:
  • Liquids
  • Laptops
  • Umbrellas
  • Luggage
  • Aerosol Sprays
  • Lighters
  • Matches
  • Box Cutters
  • Explosive Materials
  • Flammable Items
  • Tools
  • Pepper Spray/Mace
  • Sharp Objects
  • Pocket Knife
Please Note
U.S. Secret Service may prevent late-arriving delegates’ entrance into the venue. Delegates should allow additional time to clear security throughout the conference.

The list of prohibited items is subject to change without warning.
